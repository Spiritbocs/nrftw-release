3. A small floating button will appear in the corner of your screen
4. **Left-click** the button to open or close the map â€” it slides in from the left
5. **Right-click** the button to fully close the app
6. Press **Escape** to hide the map and get straight back to your game

Everything from the web version works in the desktop app, including your saved markers and cloud sync.
